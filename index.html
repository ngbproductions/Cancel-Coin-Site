What is Cancel Coin?

Cancel Coin is a satirical, edgy token that thrives on the chaos of cancel culture. It’s designed to poke at every sacred cow, rewarding holders for stirring the pot and punishing the "canceled" by burning their tokens. The idea is to gamify outrage — the more controversy it generates, the more value it accrues. It’s absurd, divisive, and unapologetic, making it a lightning rod for attention in the oversaturated meme coin space.
